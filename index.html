About Us

Responsive left-aligned hero with image

FridayAtFive is not just a band, it's a state of mind. Anytime,  anywhere it's FridayAtFive.  We are a tribute to the party. With over 38 collective years of experience among the band members touring and performing in national and international acts, FAF is a well oiled machine. 

From Van Halen to Pink, Zac Brown to Sublime, ACDC to Rihanna, Led Zeppelin to Blink 182, 4 Non Blondes to Pat Benatar,  The Killers to Bon Jovi, The Cranberries to Kings Of Leon, Calvin Harris to Lady GaGa, SRV to Elle King, we are a playlist to the party. Everyone in the band sings lead throughout the show Simona is vocalist/dancer/acrobat/showstopper, Carey provides the Shreddietastic guitar licks and Gonzalo and Jordan provide the drum & bass foundation. FridayAtFive will have you singing every word, doing every shot and dancing the night away!
